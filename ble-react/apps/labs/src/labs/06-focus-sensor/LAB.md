# Lab 06 — Steer one sensor

## Goal

Fire-and-forget `SENSOR_CFG_SET` to focus traffic on one sensor.

## On-screen steps

1. Connect.
2. Choose a sensor → **Apply focus**.

## Acceptance

CFG applied and only the focused sensor’s counter climbs → chapter complete.

## Notes

Do not wait for CFG RES. Other sensors may tick briefly until CFG lands.
